Whif AI
Decision Simulation Engine · Released on App Store
ReleasedMobileAI
Problem
People struggle to visualize the real-world consequences of a decision before making it — leading to regret, indecision, or overconfidence.
Solution
TikTok-style feed of AI-generated "life scenarios" simulating what a decision might look like. Sora video + Gemini image + narrative text scenarios, personalized to the user, served in a swipeable feed for emotional clarity before committing.
Key PM Decisions
6-channel recommendation engine (Personalized, Explore, Similar-to-Liked, Global Winners, Cold Start, Contextual) with MMR diversity selection. Semantic dedup over exact-match to prevent repetitive feeds. LRU embedding cache achieving 37% latency reduction. Feature flags for gradual rollout.

Playlist AI
One-tap playlist generator with deep taste modeling
AIMobile
What it is
Audio analysis engine extracting 30+ features per track (tempo, danceability, valence, spectral contrast, 512-dim OpenL3 embeddings) + clustering to model personal taste. One tap generates a curated playlist. Novelty slider adjusts the similarity weight (50%→30%) to balance discovery vs. familiarity. Exports to Apple Music.
Key PM Decisions
Cold start was the primary risk — solved with a pre-seeding pipeline that pulls Apple Music charts. Artist diversity penalty + previously-heard penalty + embedding similarity penalty in the ranking algorithm. Jobs table + Cloud Scheduler for async audio processing at scale.

MobileConsumer
What it is
Drop permanent AR pins at any GPS coordinate containing text, photos, or video. Pins unlock only when someone physically visits that exact spot. Color-coded by age: cyan (new <7 days), yellow (recent <30 days), red (months old), purple (years old — rare finds). Reply threads let visitors add to the pin's story over time.
